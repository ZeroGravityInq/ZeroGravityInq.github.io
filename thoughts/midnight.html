Midnight on Guard Duty

I am a security guard. Observe and report. 9 dollars hourly. 6 to 6 with half days on Friday morning.

So that leads to a lot of musing time in between patrols. Yeah, I have a co-worker I can chat with when he drops in and he's good conversation but inbetween that is musing.

Just thinking.

That's how I came up with this website, honestly. I pounded out a good couple of hours trying to think of something to occupy my mind and I fell on working on a website. A host for projects and other things that caught my mind.


I admit to being a fan of Still Drinking for no more that his sense of humor and 'I cannot begin to give a single fuck' attitude despite our opposite ends of pretty much everything aside from maybe voting Democrat and I am not 100% on that one.

Back to Security Guarding. After midnight, it gets quiet as fuck. Granted sometimes the geezers shuffle around like the Walking Dead but usually for the half shift, all is calm.

So my mind wanders.

and wanders
